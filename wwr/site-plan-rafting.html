Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this project is for us to engage in more outdoor sports with your family and friends, creating bonds and memories that will never be forgotten. With our team of professionals, you will have the most comprehensive experience in a safe and enjoyable way!

Audience

Our target audience is you and your family and friends! We want you to feel free to bring whoever you want and know that your adventure and fun are guaranteed!We use modern and high-quality equipment, and we also have affordable costs and various payment and cost-sharing options for groups. Routes of various durations, including the shortest one being 15 minutes.The minimum age to participate in our activities is 7 years old. For younger children, we have accommodation with daycare, snacks, and nap rooms, monitored by our professionals. Prior to the activity, a registration is done where the parents' names, contact phone numbers, and in case of signal loss, we have a way to communicate with the professionals who are on the tour with the guardians in case of emergency via radio.

The Whitewater Rafting Site Project is a website project that will be built throughout the course. The goal of the project is to meet the course outcomes and objectives through development specifications that match learning activities and assignments. You will be involved in the planning, design, development, and deployment of your own whitewater rafting site. Some of the pages have more prescriptive requirements that others. There will be opportunities for you to demonstrate competency and your own creativity.

White water rafting is a recreation where typically a non-motorized water craft floats and navigates down free flowing rivers. A rafting company organizes and manages trips which most often include experienced guides. The business intent is to provide a thrilling and safe experience to individuals and groups for a fee.
