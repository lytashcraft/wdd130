São Paulo, Brazil!

São Paulo constitutes the heart of the Southeast, Brazil's most developed and populous region. The state itself is the most economically productive and populous in the country, accounting for more than one-fifth of the national population.São Paulo's history began in 1554, when a Jesuit mission of the Roman Catholic order of clergy was founded. The station, which is at the heart of the current city, was named São Paulo dos Campos de Piratininga. In 1681, the captaincy was renamed São Paulo, and the town of São Paulo was designated the capital.
